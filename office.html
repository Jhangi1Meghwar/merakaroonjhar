Welcome to Tharparkar

Discover the timeless beauty of Tharparkar, where golden sands, ancient temples, and vibrant traditions create a unique tapestry of culture and nature. Immerse yourself in the rhythms of folk music, the artistry of local crafts, and the warmth of Thari hospitality. Mission & Vision

At Mera Karoonjhar, our mission is to celebrate, preserve, and promote the unique heritage, natural beauty, and cultural richness of the Karoonjhar region. We aim to connect locals and visitors alike with the land's vibrant history, fostering a sense of pride and commitment to sustainable development. Through our platform, we strive to empower communities, create opportunities, and inspire responsible tourism that protects and respects the environment and traditions of Karoonjhar.

Our vision is a thriving Karoonjhar that is recognized and celebrated worldwide for its timeless beauty, cultural depth, and ecological importance. We envision a future where the region’s heritage is preserved for generations to come, where tourism enhances local lives without compromising the natural landscape, and where Mera Karoonjhar serves as a bridge between Karoonjhar’s wonders and the global community.
